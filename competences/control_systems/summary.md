- Experimental Control (EX)
- Statistical Signal Processing — filters and estimation theory related to control
- Attended part of Nonlinear Control (not a degree subject, attended out of interest)

**Bachelor's level:**
- Control 1

Full detail: `competences/professional/summary.md`

**Experimental Control lab work:**
Implemented controllers on real mechanical systems in the lab. Hands-on experience
