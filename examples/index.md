# Examples

Reference these when writing cover letters to match tone, structure and length.
Always read the relevant example before writing a letter.



## Danfoss Cover Letter (English)
Formal header, four body paragraphs, short closing. Good example of tone —
confident and specific without overclaiming. Shows how to weave multiple
competences into flowing prose rather than listing them.
`examples/danfoss/cover_letter.md`
